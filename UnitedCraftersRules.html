United Crafters Server Rules

No NSFW Content

We strive to maintain a safe and comfortable environment for everyone. NSFW (Not Safe for Work) content is strictly -prohibited. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Sexual discussions, images, or themes.
  • Depictions or discussions of drugs, alcohol, or illegal substances.
  • Any content that an admin deems inappropriate or unsuitable for the server.

Be Respectful

Respect is the foundation of our community. Treat others with kindness and consideration. The following behaviors are not allowed:

  • Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks.
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or derogatory remarks.
  • Trolling or intentionally provoking others.

Use Appropriate Language

While casual swearing is permitted, it must not be excessive or directed at others in a harmful manner. Keep conversations respectful and suitable for all community members.

No Personal Attacks or Drama

If you have a conflict with another member, handle it privately or seek assistance from a moderator. The following are not allowed:

  • Name-calling, insults, or personal attacks.
  • Spreading rumors, gossip, or unnecessary drama.
  • Publicly accusing others of wrongdoing. Instead, use the ticket system to report concerns.

Respect Privacy

Do not share personal or private information about yourself or others without explicit permission. This includes:

  • Real-life names.
  • Phone numbers or addresses.
  • Social media accounts or private messages.
  • Any other sensitive personal data.

Follow Discords Terms of Service

All members must comply with Discords Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. Violations will be taken seriously and may result in an immediate ban.

No Spamming

Spamming disrupts the community and makes conversations difficult to follow. Avoid:

  • Excessive or repetitive messages, emojis, or reactions.
  • Flooding the chat with unnecessary content.
  • Posting links repeatedly or without context.

Stay On Topic

Each channel has a specific purpose. Keep discussions relevant to the channels topic. Misuse of channels may result in warnings or further action.

  • If a channel specifies "no chatting," create a thread instead of posting directly.
  • Repeated failure to follow channel guidelines will lead to disciplinary action.

Respect Moderators

Moderators help keep the server organized and safe. You are expected to follow their instructions. Do not:

  • Argue with or disrespect moderators.
  • Make sarcastic or rude remarks in response to warnings.
  • Challenge or undermine moderator decisions. If you disagree with a decision, use the appropriate appeal process.

No Advertising

Self-promotion is only allowed in designated areas:

  • Use the self-promotion channel for sharing personal projects.
  • Use the shop advertisement forum for server-related trades or promotions.
  • Unsolicited DMs or advertisements outside these areas will result in consequences.

Report Issues Properly

If you notice a problem, rule violation, or another issue, report it to the moderators rather than attempting to handle it yourself. Use the ticket system for:

  • Reporting rule violations.
  • Requesting moderator intervention.
  • Seeking help with disputes.

Be Positive and Contribute to the Community

Our goal is to create a fun and welcoming space. Contribute positively by:

  • Encouraging friendly discussions.
  • Supporting fellow members.
  • Providing constructive feedback via the suggestions forum.
  • Avoiding requests to make the server modded United Crafters aims to maintain a vanilla-like experience.

Welcome to the United Crafters Network

Let's introduce ourselves
UnitedCrafters is a new Semi-Vanilla SMP created by two friends who, unable to find a suitable server, decided to build their own.
The SMP will be hosted in the USA, but the owners are Australian.
If you decide to join, you are welcome to do whatever you desire, e.g., explore, build, make friends.
We don't tolerate, hacking, griefing, stealing, bullying. Violating rules like hacking, griefing, stealing, or bullying will result in punishment.
